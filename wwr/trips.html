Rio Jacaré Pepira

Brotas, known as the adventure capital of Brazil, offers thrilling rafting experiences on the Rio Jacaré Pepira. The river flows through lush landscapes, providing both excitement and natural beauty.

Rio Paraná (Foz do Iguaçu)

Experience the adrenaline rush of rafting near the stunning Iguazu Falls. The Rio Paraná offers a mix of challenging rapids and breathtaking views, making it a unique and memorable rafting destination.

Rio Mampituba (Cambará do Sul)

In the southern region of Brazil, Cambará do Sul is home to the Rio Mampituba, offering a mix of rapids suitable for various skill levels. The surrounding mountainous scenery adds an extra dimension to the rafting adventure.
